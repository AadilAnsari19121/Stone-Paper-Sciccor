How to Play

  1. Before Start Select The Rounds (Choose how many rounds you want to play, by defult its 5)
  2. Click Button For Starts! (Get ready for game!)
  3. See Dashboard
  4. Watch The Time (you have 5 seconds for 1 round)
  5. Choose Your Move: Stone Paper Scissors
  6. Computer's Turn: (Wait for computer's move)
  7. Who Wins The Round? It Will Be Show Into The Score Board (See who wins!)
  8. Final Result: (See the final your score!)

Tips

  1. Think Fast! (Choose quickly!)
  2. Watch Computer! (Watch computer's moves!)
  3. Make a Plan! (Think ahead!)
  4. Good Luck! (Have fun!)
